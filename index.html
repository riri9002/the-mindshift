Stress and Anxiety

Stress and Anxiety are both problems that affect everyone on a day to day basis. But for your age group, it seems to be a lot worse, from worrying about social interactions, to being scared for your future and college life. Managing them is extremely difficult, but in this section, we will help you combat these, to help you become the best possible version of yourself!
